# Product Hunt Launch Copy

## Headline

**Every book, turned into a living map.**

## Tagline (Product Hunt format)

> **BookMiro — Every book, turned into a living map.**
> Turns any book into a living map of characters, ideas, and connections as you read.

## Benefits

### 1. Find your reason to start

One intriguing character or idea in the map is all it takes. Follow the thread that grabs you, and the book's hidden fascination pulls you in — no more staring at page one wondering if it's worth it.

### 2. Conquer the hard ones

Dense classics, sprawling casts, tangled ideas — never lose track of who's who again. See how characters, concepts, and events connect at a glance, so the "too hard" book finally clicks.

### 3. Skip the filler, find the meat

Most books are 90% padding. The map shows you the epicenters instantly — where everything connects. All roads lead there, so you can go straight to what matters and skip the rest.

## Scannable version

- **Find your reason to start** — one character catches your eye, and you're hooked.
- **Conquer the hard books** — never lose track of who's who, or how it all connects.
- **Skip the filler** — see the epicenters instantly and jump straight to the good part.
